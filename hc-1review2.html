HC 2-1 (1) review lesson

🎯 Learning Objectives
  • Introduce my Family using “这是我...” (Zhè shì wǒ... – This is my...).
  • Tell the difference between 他 (tā – he/him) and 她 (tā – she/her).
  • Answer the question “这是谁？” (Zhè shì shéi? – Who is this?)
  • I can confidently record my own voice at the end of the lesson.
